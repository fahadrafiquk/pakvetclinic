About the Clinic

Founded in 2014 by Dr. Muhammad Haris Aziz and later jointly developed by Dr. Adnan Maqsood, Pakistan Veterinary Clinic & Store has grown into the leading animal care facility in Pākpattan and surrounding regions.

Dr. Adnan Maqsood holds a DVM and an MPhil in Clinical Medicine from UVAS. As a native of Pākpattan and a dedicated community member, he has helped transform this clinic into a trusted hub for veterinary excellence.

The clinic hosts skilled DVM graduates and well-mannered staff trained in compassionate care. We offer a broad range of procedures and diagnostic services tailored to both domestic pets and livestock.

We also offer case-to-case surgical extension services. Animal owners from across the region trust us with treatment of their valuable animals — including horses, fighting dogs, domestic and exotic cats.
